Project 10

Course Code and Name: MSJ11203: Communication and Technology.
Project Name: Online video blog.
Project Theme: Create an online blog describing yourself.
Date of Project: Summer 2021

Project Description: In this course I have learned how to make an online video in the blogspot.com website for free. We were also instructed to moderate the blog posts according to the course requirements. Our faculty provided video formatting tips for blog posts throughout the whole semester.

Project Justification: We were given the theme to create a blog on the topic to ‘Create an online blog describing myself’. I did the blog as per requirements.

Learnings and Outcomes: Through this course, I learned the fundamentals of creating and managing blogs in English. I gained an understanding of how blogs serve as a modern platform to share diverse information with the public. I acquired practical skills in setting up and organizing a blog account, as well as effectively delivering important information through well-structured blog posts. Overall, the course enhanced my ability to communicate ideas clearly and engage readers through digital content.
