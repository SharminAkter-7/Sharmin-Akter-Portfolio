Project 9

Course Code and Name: MSJ 11213: Principles of Public Relations
Project Name: Final Assignment ESSAY QUESTIONS
Date of Submission/Project: Summer 2021

Project Description: This project explores how organizations utilize the World Wide Web, social media, and corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives to enhance their public relations (PR) strategies. The study highlights the differences between traditional and digital media in reaching target audiences, the importance of proactive and reactive media relations, and the role of PR spokespersons. It also examines CSR dimensions—economic, legal, ethical, and philanthropic—and their impact on organizational reputation, sustainability, and stakeholder engagement. The project includes an analysis of effective social media channels and strategies for engaging audiences, building brands, and maintaining a positive public image.

Project Justification: The project is significant because the communication landscape has shifted from traditional media to digital platforms, where social media plays a crucial role in influencing public perception. Organizations must adapt to this shift to remain competitive, engage customers in real time, and manage crises effectively. CSR activities further enhance brand value by demonstrating ethical and social responsibility, aligning business practices with societal expectations. This project provides practical insights into:
•  How social media and the web improve PR effectiveness.
•  Best practices for media relations and spokesperson engagement.
•  Strategies for leveraging CSR to promote long-term sustainability and positive public perception.
The project helps students and professionals understand modern PR practices, the integration of digital tools, and the ethical responsibilities of organizations toward society and the environment.

Learnings and Outcomes: Through this project, I learned how the World Wide Web and social media enhance public relations by reaching audiences quickly, engaging them effectively, and measuring campaign success. I understood the roles of proactive and reactive media relations, as well as the responsibilities and rights of PR spokespersons. I also gained knowledge of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), its dimensions, types, and the importance of the triple bottom line (People, Planet, Profit) in sustainable business practices. Overall, the project helped me develop practical skills in content creation, media handling, strategic planning, and stakeholder engagement, which will support my growth in public relations and corporate communications.
