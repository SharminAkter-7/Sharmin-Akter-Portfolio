Project 5

Course Code and Name: MSJ11343 (Media Relations)
Project Name: Media Kit (Madame Tussauds Bangladesh)
Date of Submission/Project: Summer 2022

Project Description: This project focuses on the launch and promotion of Madame Tussauds Bangladesh, the 10th Asian venture of the renowned Madame Tussauds franchise. Located at Bir Uttam Mir Shawkat Sarak, Dhaka, the museum will feature 35 lifelike wax figures showcasing Bangladeshi icons, including Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, Kazi Nazrul Islam, and Mashrafe Bin Mortaza, alongside internationally recognized celebrities. The museum aims to combine entertainment and education, offering interactive sections like the “War of Heroes” to highlight freedom fighters. As part of the project, we developed a PR campaign to promote the launch, manage media coverage, and engage audiences via social media, influencer collaborations, and special events.

Project Justification: Madame Tussauds Bangladesh fills a significant gap in the country’s cultural and tourism landscape. By showcasing Bangladeshi heritage, arts, and contemporary icons, the museum provides a platform for both education and entertainment. The project leverages modern PR strategies—including social media promotion, influencer partnerships, and traditional media outreach—to attract tourists, students, and families. Collaborating with Bangladesh Shilpakala Academy ensures authenticity and cultural alignment, while the presence of a high-profile ambassador like Muhammed Zafar Iqbal enhances visibility and credibility. This initiative strengthens tourism, promotes national pride, and positions Bangladesh on the global cultural map.

Learnings and Outcomes: Through this project, I gained practical experience in planning and executing a large-scale PR and event management campaign. Key learnings include:
•   Designing campaign strategies and proposals
•   Creating content calendars, social media posts, and media kits
•   Coordinating event schedules and logistics for launch events
•   Enhancing skills in writing press releases, invitations, and promotional material
•   Understanding audience engagement, influencer collaboration, and brand positioning
Overall, the project strengthened my skills in strategic communication, digital marketing, and cultural promotion, preparing me for professional work in public relations and event management.
