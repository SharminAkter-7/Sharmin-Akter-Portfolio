Project 7

Course Code and Name: MSJ11214 Convergence Communication 1
Project Name: শিক্ষা নাকি অবিচার?
Project Theme: Non-Violent Communication/Violent Communication
Date of Project: Spring 2021

Project Description: In this project, our team focused on violent communication, specifically abusive behavior by some instructors in certain madrasas. During the pandemic, we aimed to highlight this often-ignored issue through a comic, using visual storytelling to depict the struggles students face under harsh treatment. The project involved creating character sketches, writing dialogues, and designing the final comic in Photoshop. Despite challenges and multiple revisions, we successfully completed the project, demonstrating creativity, teamwork, and adaptability under remote collaboration conditions.

Project Justification: Our main motivation was to shed light on the silent suffering of madrasa students subjected to abusive behavior, a topic rarely discussed due to social and religious sensitivities. Choosing the comic format allowed us to present this serious issue in an accessible and engaging way. By illustrating real-life interactions between students and teachers, we aimed to raise awareness, spark meaningful conversations, and advocate for a more caring, supportive, and nurturing learning environment for all children.

Learnings and Outcomes: Through this project, I learned the importance of teamwork and effective collaboration in achieving successful results. I gained valuable experience in preplanning strategies before execution, which helped streamline the creative process. My skills in Photoshop and comic creation improved significantly, along with my ability to visualize ideas beforehand. Additionally, I enhanced my team management and leadership skills while learning how to express complex emotions and sensitive issues through art. Overall, the project strengthened both my technical and interpersonal abilities, making the experience meaningful and impactful.
