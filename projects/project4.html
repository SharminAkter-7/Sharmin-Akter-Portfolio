Project 4

Course Code and Name: MSJ 11442: Public Relations Campaign.
Project Name: Part III-PR Campaign: Final Campaign Plan.
Date of Project: Summer 2022

Project Description: In this course, we developed an SDG-based PR campaign titled “Refresh the Way You Are” for Kazi & Kazi Tea, promoting their new Ice Tea as a healthy and refreshing beverage. Targeting health-conscious ULAB students, the campaign used social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok, along with hashtags like #organicrefreshment and engaging phrases such as “a taste that wakes you up.”
We partnered with health-focused influencers and popular bands like Artcell and Aurthohin, combining traditional advertising, PR strategies, and brand integration to build trust and visibility. The campaign concluded with a concert to enhance brand awareness. My role included developing the central idea, conducting a SWOT analysis, refining marketing objectives, and designing communication channels, ensuring a cohesive strategy to make Kazi & Kazi Ice Tea a top choice for students.

Project Justification: Kazi & Kazi Tea’s campaign addresses a market gap for healthy cold drinks, introducing a unique organic Ice Tea that appeals to health-conscious ULAB students and potential consumers. By analyzing customer behavior and social media trends, the campaign strategically uses platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok for maximum reach. Collaborations with popular influencers and musical bands enhance credibility, while traditional advertising, PR strategies, and brand elements create a comprehensive approach. With a carefully planned budget covering promotions, stalls, and a final concert, the campaign aligns with market needs and customer preferences, aiming to boost brand visibility, loyalty, and position Kazi & Kazi Tea as a leading choice in healthy beverages.

Learnings and Outcomes: Through this assignment, I gained hands-on experience in planning and executing a PR campaign, learning the key aspects that contribute to its success. I developed skills in creating comprehensive campaign proposals and strategies, building guidelines and task lists, and preparing budgets. I also improved my knowledge of content calendars, Gantt charts, and the creation of scripts, social media posts, and engaging captions. Additionally, I learned how to design effective posters that visually communicate the campaign’s message. Overall, this project enhanced my practical understanding of campaign management, digital content creation, visual communication, and strategic planning, preparing me for future PR initiatives.
