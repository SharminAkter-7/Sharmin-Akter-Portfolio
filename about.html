About Me

Sharmin Akter

Major In Public Relations · University of Liberal Arts Bangladesh (ULAB)

Since childhood, I’ve been energized by the simple joy of meeting new people and understanding the stories they carry. That curiosity gradually shaped my dream of becoming a skilled Communicator—someone who not only speaks but listens, connects, and creates meaningful thoughts. Choosing ULAB was a deliberate step toward that dream, and every year here has strengthened my passion for learning, experimenting, and growing.

My website is a reflection of that journey. It showcases a diverse collection of my work: research papers, posters, campaign designs, mask-making projects, video art, feature writing, advertisements, press releases, comic art, blogs, and various productions. Some of the projects that resonated with me the most came from courses like Mass Communication, Convergence of Communication I, and my research work. Beyond academics, hands-on tasks such as creating advertisements, producing videos, experimenting with Photoshop, developing media kits, and managing internal communication helped me understand the real rhythm of media work—its demands, its intensity, and its excitement.

Each project taught me something new. They strengthened my communication abilities, sharpened my writing, deepened my understanding of media practices, and helped me learn how to stay calm and efficient under pressure. More importantly, these experiences allowed me to blend theory with practice, transforming classroom lessons into real skills.

Altogether, my journey at ULAB hasn’t just shaped me academically—it has shaped me personally. I now approach communication with confidence, creativity, and a readiness to keep learning from every opportunity that comes my way.
